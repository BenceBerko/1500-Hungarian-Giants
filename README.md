# 1500-Hungarian-Giants
Data science project for analyzing the performance of the 1500 biggest Hungarian companies in 2024

Insights from Data: 
The big picture: how companies accounting for nearly half of the Hungarian economy performed in 2024.

We often hear about the largest corporations, but we rarely see the big picture all at once-how their performance is distributed across the country or between various sectors. I examined the 2024 data of the 1500 largest Hungarian companies by revenue and leveraged data science to create a chart for investors, decision-makers, executives, financial professionals, and enthusiasts that is readable, clear, and comprehensive.

The result is an interactive chart where anyone can browse through regional (county-level) and industry-specific data.

The Process:
Data Collection and Cleaning (Google Sheets, Python, SQL)
Analysis and Modeling (SQL, Python)
Data Visualization (Python, Looker Studio)
